What is Fusionary?

Fusionary is a collaborative platform for music performers and artists across genres. It allows users to come together to create and collaborate on music. The platform is not just for collaboration, but also serves as a one-stop-shop for music exploration. Users can upload their own music, attend events, learn new skills, and push their boundaries in the musical world of Fusionary.
